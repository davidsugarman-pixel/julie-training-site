About Julie

I'm an ISSA-certified personal trainer and active NPC competitor based in Bellevue, WA. I competed at the Emerald Cup 2025 — so when I write your program, it comes from someone who actually walks the walk.

Whether your goal is losing weight, building lean muscle, working around a health limitation, or stepping on stage yourself — I build programs that are challenging, safe, and specific to you. No cookie-cutter templates.

I train clients in-person across the Bellevue area and coach online clients worldwide. My approach combines strength training, nutrition strategy, and the accountability that actually gets you results.
